Salagdoong Beach: A Thrill-Seeker’s Paradise

Located on the eastern coast of Siquijor, Salagdoong Beach is one of the island’s most exciting attractions. Famous for its high cliff-diving spots, this beach is perfect for adrenaline junkies looking for an adventure. The crystal-clear turquoise waters and powdery white sand make it a scenic destination for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing.

The beach is part of a government-run resort, offering basic amenities like cottages and picnic areas. If you’re not into cliff diving, you can still enjoy the beauty of Salagdoong by kayaking along the shore or simply relaxing under the shade of lush trees. The waters here are exceptionally clear, allowing visitors to spot marine life even without diving deep.

Whether you’re looking for heart-racing adventure or a peaceful beach escape, Salagdoong Beach offers the best of both worlds.
